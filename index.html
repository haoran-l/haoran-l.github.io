Ph.D. Candidate in Accountancy

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Accountancy at CUHK Business School. Before joining CUHK in 2024, I earned an M.Phil. and a B.B.A. in Accounting from Shanghai University of Finance and Economics. My research examines how disclosure and information intermediaries shape market efficiency and corporate real activities, with particular interest in the information-processing costs that connect capital-market signals to corporate decision-making.
