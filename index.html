Research agenda

Questions that connect information to action.

My interests sit at the intersection of disclosure, information processing, and regulation. I am especially interested in how information environments change behavior inside firms and across capital markets.

01

Disclosure and real effects

How disclosure requirements alter operating, investment, and organizational choices beyond their immediate capital-market effects.

02

Information intermediaries

How intermediaries process complex information, and how processing costs shape the speed and quality of market learning.

03

Regulation and welfare

How the design and implementation of regulation redistribute information, incentives, and welfare across stakeholders.
