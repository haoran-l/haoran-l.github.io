Research

Information, incentives, and real decisions.

My research interests focus on the real effects of disclosure, information intermediaries and processing costs, and the welfare consequences of regulation.

Current themes

Real effects of disclosure

I am interested in when disclosure changes decisions rather than merely prices—particularly how reporting rules influence firms, managers, and other stakeholders.

DisclosureReal effectsFirm decisions

Information intermediaries and processing costs

I study how the capacity and incentives of information intermediaries affect the way complex information is interpreted and incorporated into markets.

IntermediariesProcessing costsMarket efficiency

Regulation and social welfare

I am interested in evaluating regulation beyond compliance outcomes, including how implementation affects incentives, resource allocation, and welfare.

RegulationWelfareImplementation
